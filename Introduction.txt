1 论文：DP-PSE: Lightweight Dual-Path Intrusion Detection via Physical Semantic Embedding for In-Vehicle Networks
2.采用架构：his paper proposes DP-PSE, a natively lightweight intrusion detection system (IDS) utilizing a dual-path architecture. First, a T-shaped sliding window mechanism is designed to simultaneously capture macroscopic temporal dependencies of bus traffic and the microscopic physical state continuity of specific nodes. Second, Physical Semantic Embedding (PSE) maps discrete hexadecimal message identifiers (IDs) into a high-dimensional continuous latent space, realizing the mathematical modeling of implicit topologies and functional clustering at the Electronic Control Unit (ECU) level. For feature extraction, a parallel dual-path inference engine is constructed: the temporal path utilizes a lightweight Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) network to monitor the evolutionary inertia of physical signals, while the semantic path employs Multi-Head Attention to precisely capture local semantic mutations
